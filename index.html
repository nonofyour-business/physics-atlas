Physics atlas

Concepts that connect.

Mechanics

Forces explain changes in motion.

Newton's second law links net force to acceleration: F = ma.

Energy

Energy is the accounting system.

Kinetic, potential, thermal, and field energy transform but do not vanish.

Waves

Patterns carry energy without carrying matter along.

Frequency, wavelength, and speed fit together as v = fλ.

Fields

Space can store instructions.

Fields assign a value to each point, telling charges or masses how to respond.

Relativity

Measurements depend on motion.

Time and distance adjust so every observer measures the same light speed.

Quantum

Small systems predict probabilities.

The wavefunction gives a probability pattern, not a tiny hidden marble path.
